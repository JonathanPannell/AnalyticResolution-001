Requirements Management

 

"When will you make an end?"

 

Requirements are the key to the delivery of any solution. While a new project is created, often the requirements evolve as we see new opportunities or discover areas which were initially thought as unaffected.

 

Keeping track of "what is needed", rather than "what is desired" will allow you to state with confidence "when you will finish"

 

  • Requirements Traceability Matrix
    • Agreement, classification and the level of Importance for every requirement and every requirement owners
    • Each requirements is audited and monitored so the development and verification of each new feature is provided to the requirement owner and programme in real time.
    • Test witnessing, Internal UAT buy in and external UAT sign off

 

  • Test Coverage Traceability Matrix
    • For a programme release, there can be multiple IT work streams with co dependencies on each other. Knowing "How to Test"  and "When to Test" is key to reporting the ongoing quality of each work stream and the work from each Supplier.
    • Real Time reporting of where the project is and when it will finish

 

  • Defect Tracking
    • Ownership of the Defect Management Review Board
    • Defect Tracking Processes and Defect Management Software
    • Defects classified with Severity and Priority ratings, verified by the requirement and business owners.
    •  Defect Tracking with step by step guides to recreate the issue
    •  Watch a screen captured movie recording of the defect from start to finish.
    • Real time defect tracking reporting, Open, Closed and In Progress defects reported by Supplier, Severity and Priority.
